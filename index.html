GAME RULES:
1. You will be given a random number at the start of a game.
2. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your score.
3. You win by matching your total score to random number, you lose if score goes over random number.
4. The value of each crystal in hidden from you until you click on it.
5. Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
